About Us

Asidleni Café is a family-owned restaurant located in Soweto, South Africa. Serving traditional African cuisine for over 20 years, Asidleni is loved for its homestyle dishes like umngqusho, chakalaka, and bobotie. We are excited to bring our experience online with our new digital presence.
